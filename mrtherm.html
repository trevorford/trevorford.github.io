Mr. Therm

Commercial Refrigeration Temperature Monitoring

Circuit Cellar article can be found here

During the fall of 2018 as part of the Electronic and Computer Engineering Technology program at Camosun College, I worked in a group of four to complete a capstone project. We built a system to simultaneously monitor all of the refrigeration units in a commericial kitchen. The system automatically logs the temperature information from all units for health inspections, and alerts the user if the unit reaches an unsafe temperature. We utulized a Raspberry Pi for the main screen and PIC microcontrollers for the monitoring units. My primary task was designing the hardware for the project. I designed four seperate circuit boards in total: Two interchangable power supply boards, a raspberry pi "hat", and the satellite unit board all of which are pictured below.
